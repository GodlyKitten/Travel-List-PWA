# Travel-List-PWA
A PWA for a travel list, an app for NOT forgetting your essentials on your dream travels!!!
Have you ever gone on a trip and forgotten the most important thing you may have needed? Well, with this PWA, you get an app that can make checklists for you to make SURE you will never forget the things you may have forgotten to pack, you can even edit or duplicate lists after they have been done. I PROMISE with this app, all your packing and remembering troubles will go away. This is version 0.1.
